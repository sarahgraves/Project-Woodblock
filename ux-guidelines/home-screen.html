Home Screen

The home screen is an opportunity to present the app's primary value propositions as well as any merchandised content that our programming team would like to promote. Not all devices will support a home screen. For instance, the type of browsing that the home screen encourages is not appropriate in an automotive application.

Personalization

Returning users who are authenticated with an iHeartRadio account should be greeted with a quick way to listen to their most recent stations.

Featured Content Carousel

The featured content carousel will display up to five hand created slides. Each slide will contain the following components:

  • An image that will be dynamically cropped by the backend to whatever aspect ratio the app requires.
  • A headline with a maximum of 60 characters. ***verify***
  • A Subheadline. This is not required on devices with a limited screen size.
  • A prompt to listen to the associated radio station, e.g., if the item is promoting a contest for Rhianna tickets, the prompt should lead the user to Rhianna Radio.
  • A call to action, e.g., entering a contest or reading an article. Devices that do not support the specified CTA should not display the associated item.

Recently Played

Many iHeartRadio listeners only listen to a handful of stations. Users should be able to return to a recently-listened-to station with minimal interaction required. Recently Played should display between 5 and 8 stations and be listed in reverse chronological order. The Recently Played section should contain a link to the user's list of saved stations in My Stations.

Stations Near You

Live Stations from the market nearest the user's location should be prominantly displayed on the home screen. At least 3 to 6 stations should be displayed in order of CUME with a link to a full list.

Featured Custom Artist Stations

This list is hand curated by our programming team. 3 to 6 Custom stations should be displayed. This is an important opportunity to communicate the functionality of a Custom station. Each station listing should be accompanied by a list of artists who's music will play on that station titled "Featuring music from:"

iHeartRadio Originals

A few of the most recently added iHeartRadio Originals should be listed on the home screen.
