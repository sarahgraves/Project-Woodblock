Value Propositions / Experience Outcomes

Primary Value Propositions

The most important value proposition that iHeartRadio brings to its users is the ability to listen their favorite broadcast radio stations on any internet connected device they choose. iHeartRadio also provides users the opportunity to interact with their stations in unique, compelling ways and to extend their enthusiasm for radio by creating new radio stations that tailor to users' specific tastes and needs. Finally, iHeartRadio provides ancillary media such as photos, videos, and articles, that enrich users' listening experience.

Experience Outcomes

  • First and foremost, users should be able to quickly and easily locate and play their favorite terrestrial radio station. Most users will prefer to make use of search functionality, but browse functionality should be provided for users who prefer it and highlighted on platforms where text entry is problematic.
  • Users should be educated about and encouraged to engage in our other product features such as Custom Radio and iHeartRadio Originals
  • Once they have begun listening to a station, users should be exposed to a compelling experience by viewing artist bios, song lyrics, photos & videos. This experience should enhance user engagement and extend listening hours.
  • Users should find it easy and seamless to share their listening activity on their social network of choice.
  • Users should feel that iHeartRadio is, simple, straightforward, and welcoming to a broad audience.
  • Advertising integrations should be appropriate and compelling, giving users a clear chance to interact with the advertised brand.
